Air Filters

Home air filters should be changed regularly to ensure proper removal of dust and allergens in the air. If you have furry friends consider changing filters monthly; quarterly if only humans at home.

Dryer Vents

Dryer lint can build up over time and can become a fire hazard. Be sure to periodically pull the dryer away from the wall, disconnect the hose from the back of the unit to vacuum the hose and vent.

Gutters - Cleaning

With all of the beautiful trees come all of the beautiful leaves that can fall into our gutters.

Gutters - Downspout Diverters

Charlotte sees a lot of rain throughout the year but especially during the winter and spring months. Be sure that your gutters have downspout diverters that direct the water away from your home's foundation
